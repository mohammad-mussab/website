Who Am I ?

About Me

Hi there! I'm Muhammad Mussab, a 17-year-old student from Daska, Punjab, Pakistan. After finishing my matriculation, I'm now stepping into college life.
A couple of years ago, I tried my hand at graphic design and cybersecurity, but it wasn't until I watched a video by Irfan Malik on YouTube that I discovered my true passion for artificial intelligence. Since then, I've been hooked on AI, especially machine learning and data science. I've learned a lot in the past year and am excited to keep learning more.
Outside of tech, I love hitting the gym, reading books, watching YouTube, and sometimes playing Free Fire. I believe that when you love something and put in the effort, success will follow.
